Yellow Mango Recipe

Ingredients

  • 5 oz carrots - chopped
  • 1 apple - chopped
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 3 tbsp walnuts
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup ice
  • 1 spoon full of sugar

A little taste of Fall, this high fiber smoothie should restore any slight digestion issues! Apples contain a lot of fiber per serving (about 4g in a medium-sized fruit), as do carrots. Additionally, carrots are bursting with beta-carotene and biotin, making them a great food for your skin. Full of vitamin E and Omega-3s, walnuts are also fantastic for keeping skin supple and healthy.
